Texto Camisetería sentimental

Registro de la exposición por Valeria Giraldo, aquí

43 artistas colombianos rinden tributo a diferentes bandas y músicos mediante camisetas impresas en serigrafía. Las camisetas fueron impresas en Rat Trap, a cada participante se le dio un lote para distribuir como quisiera.



Conceived by Valeria Giraldo, Humberto Junca, Rat Trap and José Sanín. An adaptation of Piratería sentimental a proyect by Humberto Junca
Rat Trap
Bogotá
2015

Texto Camisetería sentimental

43 colombian artists pay tribute to different bands and musicians by means of bootleg screen printed t-shirts designed by themselves. The shirts were printed at Rat Trap and each artist was given a batch to distribute as they wished.
